    Model United Nations (MUN) is an educational simulation activity that involves students role-playing as delegates to the United Nations and other international organizations.     Indian Institute of Information Technology Guwahati (IIITG) is a premier institute of higher education located in Guwahati, the capital city of the northeastern Indian state of Assam. Established in 2013, IIITG is one of the 20 Indian Institutes of Information Technology (IIITs) established by the Indian Government as institutions of national importance.
